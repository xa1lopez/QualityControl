Detailed Description

The class in charge of running the checks on a MonitorObject.

A Checker is in charge of loading/instantiating the proper checks for a given MonitorObject, to configure them and to run them on the MonitorObject in order to generate a quality. At the moment, a checker also stores the MO and its quality in the repository.
